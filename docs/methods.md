# Methods

The paper studies destination allocation conditional on the next retained fixation transition being forward, within sentence, and within line. Corpus conversion, candidate construction, nuisance specifications, split-half estimation, simulations, auxiliary supervision, and cross-corpus transfer are defined in the [manuscript](../manuscript/manuscript.md). Exact frozen commands and randomization counts, including the 500 shared destination-permutation replicates, are listed in [reproducibility.md](reproducibility.md).

The reusable implementation is under `src/eyetrack2llm/`; command-line analyses are under `scripts/`. Published tables, source data, figures, and their SHA-256 provenance are under `manuscript/`. No scientific result is recomputed by the default release verification.
